Benefit Administration

Accessing your benefits, made simple

We administer all retirement and terminal benefits entirely FREE of charge. Here's how you can access your funds when it matters most.

Retirement

Full access to your RSA balance upon reaching retirement age (50 years+) or after mandatory 35 years of service.

Programmed Withdrawal

Systematic monthly payments from your RSA after retirement — ensuring a steady income stream for life.

Temporary Access (25%)

Access up to 25% of your RSA if you've been unemployed for 4+ months or for a residential mortgage down payment.

Death Benefits

Full RSA balance paid to nominated beneficiaries, plus life insurance proceeds from the employer — processed with care and urgency.

Home Ownership

Use up to 25% of your RSA as an equity contribution towards a residential mortgage. Home ownership just got closer.

Medical Reasons

Withdraw your full RSA balance if you suffer a permanent disability or critical illness certified by a qualified physician.

En Bloc

Lump-sum payment for retirees whose RSA balance is insufficient to procure a programmed withdrawal or annuity plan.

Cross Border

Special arrangements for non-Nigerian expatriates who have worked in Nigeria and wish to repatriate their RSA balance.

FAQs

Common questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Our team is always ready to help.

View All FAQs
I haven't registered with a PFA yet — how do I join?
Simply open an account online at web.fcmbpensions.com/onlineenrollment or download our RSA Account Opening Form and visit any of our branch offices. View branch locations on our Contact Us page.
Can I transfer my RSA from another PFA to FCMB Pensions?
Yes — RSA holders are permitted to transfer to another PFA once every 12 months during a transfer window. Visit fcmbpensions.com/RSA-transfer-window to initiate your transfer today.
How can I update my account details online?
Log in to your account at web.fcmbpensions.com/clientportal, then navigate to Bio Data → Update. You can update your phone number, email address, and residential address directly. You'll receive a confirmation notification immediately.
